Introduction

文章[Benaloh86]介绍了Shamir门限方案的同态性质，提出了 kkk -一致性的概念，进而提出了组合门限方案，并给出了应用：可验证秘密共享和电子投票。

The Homomorphism Property

  Shamir in [SHA79] defines a (k,n)(k,n)(k,n) threshold scheme to be a division of a secret DDD into nnn pieces D1,⋯ ,DnD_1, \cdots,D_nD1​,⋯,Dn​, in such a way that:

  1. knowledge of any kkk or more DiD_iDi​ pieces makes DDD easily computable;
  2. knowledge of any k−1k-1k−1 or fewer DiD_iDi​ pieces leaves DDD completely undetermined (in the sense that all its possible values are equally likely).

  Shamir门限方案有两个条件，一是等于或多于kkk方的情况，一是少于kkk人的情况，满足同态性质，定义如下：

  需要注意的是，原始的Sharmir门限方案只满足 (+,+)(+,+)(+,+) 同态。原因是 ddd 个 t−1t-1t−1 次多项式相加，仍是 t−1t-1t−1 次，而 ddd 个 t−1t-1t−1 次多项式相乘，结果为 d(t−1)d(t-1)d(t−1) 次。根据拉格朗日插值定理，至少需要 d(t−1)+1d(t-1)+1d(t−1)+1 个份额才能恢复出秘密，可能会出现 d(t−1)+1>nd(t-1)+1>nd(t−1)+1>n ，即拥有所有份额都无法恢复秘密的情况。

BGW协议[BGW88]中的加法运算，就是利用Shamir门限方案的加法同态实现秘密值和的安全多方计算。

  但是通过离散对数，可以将其构造成 (×,+)(\times,+)(×,+)-composite threshold scheme. By using a homomorphism between addition and discrete logarithms, for example, it is possible to transform Shamir’s scheme into a (×,+)(\times,+)(×,+)-composite (k,n) threshold scheme.即：The sum of the shares of the discrete logs of the secrets are shares of the discrete log of the product of the secrets.

Composite threshold scheme

Definition

Verifiable Secret Sharing

 可验证的秘密共享的问题是要说服份额持有者，他们的份额（集体）是 kkk 一致（ kkk-consistent）的，即任意 k∈nk\in nk∈n 个份额都能恢复出同一个secret。

Definition We say that a set of nnn shares s1,s2,⋯ ,sns_1,s_2,\cdots, s_ns1​,s2​,⋯,sn​ is kkk-consistent if every subset of kkk of the nnn shares defines the same secret.

 Note that: Shamir's scheme, the shares s1,s2,⋯ ,sns_1,s_2,\cdots,s_ns1​,s2​,⋯,sn​ are kkk - consistent if and only if the interpolation of the points (1,sl),(2,s2),⋯ ,(n,sn)(1,s_l),(2,s_2),\cdots,(n,s_n)(1,sl​),(2,s2​),⋯,(n,sn​) yields a polynomial of degree at most d=k−1d = k - 1d=k−1. It is also useful to observe that if the sum of two polynomials is of degree at most ddd, then either both are of degree at most ddd or both are of degree greater than ddd.

VSS思路

图中主要思路是一种交互式的协议，prover加密了 nnn 个（假设100个）多项式发送给verifier，verifier从中随机选择50个验证多项式的度（假设这些多项式的度都至多为 ddd，那有理由相信剩下的50个多项式的度也为 ddd）。思路用了 kkk 一致性的定义，即由 nnn 个不同点处的值给出的多项式 PPP 的degree至多为 ddd，则表示是同一个秘密所生成的多项式，自然也就恢复出同一个秘密。
